MGRS coordinates are a single string composed of a pattern of letters and numbers representing a grid square.</p><p>The MGRS Map Tool displays MGRS coordinates in the QGIS status bar (bottom left) as you pan and zoom around the map. The Zoom to MGRS coordinate tool allow you to zoom the map canvas to a given MGRS coordinate.</p><p>This plugin is available with a Boundless Desktop&nbsp;&mdash Basic or higher Subscription <a href="https://connect.boundlessgeo.com/UserLogin?returnurl=%2fPurchase-Boundless-Desktop" style="font: bold; text-decoration: none; background-color: #fdcc34; color: #333333; padding: 2px 6px 2px 6px;">Get&nbsp;access</a></p>
